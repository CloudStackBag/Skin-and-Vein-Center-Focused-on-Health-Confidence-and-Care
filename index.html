SERVICES OFFERED

Skin and Aesthetic Treatments

For skin concerns and cosmetic care, patients can access a variety of treatments to achieve healthy, radiant skin:

  • Laser Skin Resurfacing: Reduces fine lines, scars, and uneven pigmentation for a smoother complexion.
  • Facial Vein Removal: Improves the appearance of visible veins on the face.
  • Rosacea and Acne Treatments: Addresses underlying causes to support healthier, clearer skin.
  • PRP Facial Treatments: Use the body’s own growth factors to rejuvenate skin and restore a youthful glow.
  • Scar Revision & Stretch Mark Treatments: Helps improve skin texture and reduce the appearance of marks over time.
  • Other Skin Services: Includes benign lesion removal, red light therapy, and plantar wart treatment for comprehensive skin care.

Each procedure is tailored to individual skin type, goals, and lifestyle, with pre- and post-treatment guidance to maximize results.

Vein Treatments

Specialized vein care combines medical and cosmetic procedures to improve circulation, reduce discomfort, and restore confidence. Key vein treatments include:

  • Endovenous Laser Treatment (EVLT): A minimally invasive procedure for varicose veins, offering quick recovery and effective results.
  • Ultrasound-Guided Foam Sclerotherapy: Targets larger and smaller veins with precision to reduce visible veins and discomfort.
  • Visual Sclerotherapy: Focuses on spider veins to improve appearance and help patients feel more confident.
  • Other Vein Care: Treatments for restless leg syndrome, nocturnal leg cramps, vulvar varicosities, and venous stasis ulcers, all aimed at improving circulation and overall vascular health.

Each treatment plan is customized, with step-by-step guidance from experienced physicians to ensure safe and lasting results.

How to Prepare for Your Consultation

Before your visit, taking a few steps can help make the consultation productive and ensure the best treatment plan:

  • Compile your medical history: Include previous procedures, medications, and any chronic conditions.
  • List concerns and goals: Clearly note the areas you want to address, whether cosmetic or health-related.
  • Review insurance options: Bring relevant information, as most insurance plans are accepted.
  • Prepare questions: Ask about procedures, recovery times, and what to expect from treatments.

Being prepared helps the physicians understand your needs fully and allows them to recommend the most effective treatments, from vein therapies to skin rejuvenation and functional medicine services.

COMMON QUESTIONS ABOUT TREATMENTS AND CARE

  1. What types of services are offered?
    The clinic provides specialized medical and cosmetic treatments for veins and skin. Services include vein therapy, laser treatments, and skin rejuvenation procedures.
  2. How is the right treatment determined?
    A consultation with board-certified physicians ensures a personalized plan. Medical history, current health, skin concerns, and treatment goals are all considered.

TIPS FOR MAINTAINING HEALTHY VEINS AND SKIN

Even after treatment, maintaining vein and skin health is crucial. Recommended steps include:

  • Regular exercise: Promotes circulation and vein health.
  • Balanced diet: Rich in fiber and low in sodium to reduce inflammation and swelling.
  • Hydration: Keeps skin elastic and supports overall vascular health.
  • Compression stockings:
    Helps prevent progression of varicose veins if recommended.
  • Sun protection: Reduces skin damage, pigmentation, and aging.

Following these recommendations supports long-term results and overall wellness.
